Our Heritage

From a Street Cart
to Jhansi's Heart

It started with a single cart, one iron tawa, and an old family recipe passed down through generations. Narayan ji set up his humble chaat stall on the bustling lanes of Jhansi — and the aroma alone stopped people in their tracks.

Today, decades later, that same soul still simmers in every bowl of papdi chaat, every crispy moong dal chilla, every tangy golgappa we serve. We haven't just made food — we've made memories for families across Jhansi.

1985

Narayan ji starts with a small street cart near Jhansi's old market

1998

First permanent stall established — queues stretch around the corner

2012

Expanded with a full menu, second-generation recipes introduced

Today

Serving 500+ customers daily — Jhansi's most loved chaat destination
